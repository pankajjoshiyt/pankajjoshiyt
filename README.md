## 🧑‍💻 About Me

> **Embedded Systems Engineer** and **The Electronics Creator** — I design circuits, write firmware, and build projects that bridge the physical and digital world. From bare-metal STM32 to IoT-connected ESP32 systems, I share everything on my YouTube channel **@TheElectronicsCreator**.

- 🔭 Currently working on **ESP32-based IoT systems** and **RTOS firmware projects**
- 🌱 Exploring **FPGA design**, **Zephyr RTOS**, and **Edge AI on microcontrollers**
- 📺 Creating tutorials on **Arduino**, **Raspberry Pi**, **PCB Design**, and **Embedded C**
